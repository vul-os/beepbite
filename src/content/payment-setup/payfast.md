# PayFast Setup Instructions

> **PayFast support coming soon — this provider is currently inactive.**

BeepBite plans to add full PayFast integration in an upcoming release. When available, the setup will follow a similar process to Paystack and Stripe:

1. Retrieve your Merchant ID and Merchant Key from the [PayFast Merchant Portal](https://www.payfast.co.za/merchant).
2. Add your BeepBite webhook URL to PayFast's ITN (Instant Transaction Notification) settings.
3. Paste your credentials into BeepBite and click **Save**.

In the meantime, you can accept payments via **Paystack** or **Stripe**, or enable **on-delivery payment** options (cash / card machine) from the section below.

If PayFast integration is critical for your business, please contact support so we can prioritise it.
